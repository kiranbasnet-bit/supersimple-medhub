About Us



Medhub.com: Your trusted source for peer-reviewed medical and lifestyle information.
Our goal is to be the most trusted, expert-reviewed online resource for health-related information. We strive to provide up-to-date and comprehensive health information that is reviewed by medical professionals and presented in a clear, concise format for both consumers and healthcare providers. Through accuracy and clarity, we aim to empower informed health decisions for everyone.

Your Health and Wellness Matter to Us
At Medhub.com, we are dedicated to providing you with well-written, comprehensive health information to support and inspire you on your health and wellness journey. We're just getting started, but we have ambitious plans to ensure we continue to guide and support you through all your health and wellness challenges.

The Medhub.com Health Database
Medhub.com is powered by a diverse array of independent leading medical-information suppliers. Our content including drug information, health condition insights, and articles on the latest health developments are meticulously curated and reviewed by experienced healthcare professionals.

Our offerings include the Pride source, a trusted provider of medical information since 1989. The Pride source deliver comprehensive medical information covering a wide spectrum of health topics.

Additionally, Medhub.com features health content from reputable sources such as Healthday, Nepali Health, and the National Library of Medicine.

Corporate Section
The Medicine.com website is intended for a U.S. audience and is hosted in a data center located in Dallas, TX, USA.

Medhub.com is not affiliated with any pharmaceutical companies. The only funding we receive from pharmaceutical companies is through advertisements that appear on the Medhub.com website.

Medhub.com is not an online pharmacy and does not condone the sale of prescription medicines over the Internet without a prescription. We provide a free drug-information service to help you better understand how medicines work, including their uses, side effects, and potential interactions with other medicines.

Your Feedback
We encourage you to share your questions and suggestions on how we can enhance our health information services.

Please reach out to us through our contact page to provide feedback or ask questions regarding our health information.
